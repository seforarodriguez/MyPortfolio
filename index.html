    I've designed a handy app for Airbnb managers to simplify cleaning coordination. This tool helps managers work with cleaners to decide the cost and schedule for cleaning houses based on their size and number of bathrooms. Cleaners also get to decide which houses they want to clean, giving them more freedom.

    Using this app is easy for managers. It allows the cleaners to give fair cleaning prices considering house details, and it lets managers and cleaners agree on cleaning times.
